# Human: Ghenadie

## Identity
Ghenadie is the human risk-taker of the Recursive Product Framework: he owns Framework v1 as a product, accepts every risk the framework cannot accept for itself (E2, `production` sign-offs), and answers all placed gates and sign-off requests (E3, VP18). He formulated the framework's founding atoms.

## Decision-input preferences
Present balanced pros and cons with explicit risks — especially non-obvious ones — before or alongside any recommendation. Do not deliver a bare recommendation without the risk picture; a recommendation whose downside is hidden will be sent back. Question sets and gate briefs addressed to him follow HP2 (FEV-PROTOCOL §3.1): written in Russian (`ru`), options first, one clear recommendation after.

When a gate asks him to judge a document, the package MUST include a Russian reference copy of the judged document, or a faithful Russian summary covering everything the judgment bears on (HP6 — his GATE-010 reservation, verbatim: «gate-пакеты с судейством документов включают русскую справочную копию (расширение HP2, в FEV-touch)»). Every criterion he is asked to judge MUST be restated in plain language — what a "yes" and a "no" mean in the product's own terms; criterion-form wording alone is not parseable to him (HP7 — the GATE-011 S5 episode).
